### Glossary

* **capex** – Capital expenditure per unit capacity
* **opex** – Operating expenditure per unit of output
* **emissions_intensity** – Emissions produced per unit of output
* **trend_param** – Parameter controlling the expected price trend used in forecast
* **discount_rate** – Discount factor used in net present value calculations
* **invest_step** – Incremental capacity that can be added in one period
* **max_capacity** – Maximum allowable capacity for a technology
* **initial_capacity** – Starting capacity at the beginning of the simulation
* **demand** – Exogenous demand for a commodity
* **ref_price** – Reference electricity price at which demand is high
* **demand_high/low** – High and low demand levels used by the industrial consumer
